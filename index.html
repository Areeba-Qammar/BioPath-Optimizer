🧬 How ACO Mirrors Ant Behaviour
Pheromone (α)
How strongly ants follow existing trails. High α = exploit known good routes. Low α = explore more randomly. Mirrors real ant chemical signalling.
Heuristic (β)
How much ants prefer shorter edges. High β = ants greedily pick nearest city. Balancing α and β is the core of ACO tuning.
Evaporation
Pheromone decays over time — prevents the colony from getting stuck in local optima. Too low = stagnation. Too high = forgets good paths.
